About

João Pargana is both artist and scientist. He studied piano at HfMDK Frankfurt followed by computer science in the Goethe-University of the same city. His broad range of interests are reflected in computer science where he has learned multiple languages, and is fascinated with topics starting from embedded hardware systems and pentesting to web and mobile development.

His technical background in music manifests itself in his approach to learn and practice any skill with lots of dedication in his private time. Besides his professional fields, João is particularly interested in linguistics, which is shown by his fluency in the english, portuguese, german, russian and spanish language, as well as his attitude towards machine learning, especially natural language processing.
